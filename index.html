About Sunny's Grill

Founded in 2009, Sunny's Grill started with a simple goal: serve high-quality comfort food without sacrificing speed, freshness, or flavor.

Our menu features flame-grilled burgers, crispy chicken sandwiches, signature bowls, loaded appetizers, and bottomless sides. Whether you're stopping by for lunch or grabbing dinner with family and friends, Sunny's Grill is built around great food and great experiences.
